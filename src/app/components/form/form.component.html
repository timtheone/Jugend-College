Do you want to work on your future and have better opportunities? There are four steps to get started on our individual classes with JugendCollege:

1. Booking In

There are two ways to get into the JugendCollege: Either the AMS or the Fonds Soziales Wien/Ma17 can book you onto your track for the JugendCollege.

2. Introductory phase

For your individual consulting, your background will be checked and your experience evaluated. Your advisor will then create a trailored program for you.

3. Attend classes

Core Modules: German classes
Special Modules: Critical participation, Alphabetization in native language
Extracurricular offers: Social Activities, Professional orientation consulting
