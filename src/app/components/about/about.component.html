Jugend College is an Austria-wide provider of high quality education and consulting services.
Our core competence lies in the area of ​​business-related, personality-oriented, labor market policy and socially inclusive programs of all sizes.
We carry out a large number of projects and educational programs on behalf of the Austrian Public Employment Service (AMS) or other public clients. Relevant information can be found in the menu item "Work & occupation" .
In the menu "Courses" you will find our education and training programs that we offer both for public clients and for private individuals.
Our own area of ​​responsibility is our commitment in the area of "AQUA" . Here we offer services in the area of ​​personnel recruiting and personnel development.
